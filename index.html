About Us

Kalye Roxas Shawarma House

Nestled in the heart of Barangay 4, Roxas Street, Dolores, Eastern Samar, Kalye Roxas Shawarma House is a cozy and inviting restaurant known for its signature shawarma and delightful menu of foods and beverages. The name "Kalye Roxas" draws inspiration from its location, giving the restaurant a sense of identity deeply rooted in the community.

Since its establishment in 2021, Kalye Roxas Shawarma House has become a go-to spot for locals and visitors alike, celebrated for its unique take on shawarma that combines bold flavors, fresh ingredients, and exceptional quality. Whether you're craving a savory wrap, a refreshing drink, or just looking for a warm place to enjoy great food, Kalye Roxas Shawarma House offers a culinary experience that's both satisfying and memorable.
